Wallet Wiz

Overview

The growing popularity of digital banking and fintech companies have resulted in the break down of trust that younger generations have with traditional banking. Banks are not keeping up with the demands of more personalized experiences and inclusivity. The goal of this study is to increase customer engagement in young adults by catering to their curiosity for self-discovery and self-improvement. This design is a mobile app quiz that gains a deeper understanding of its users personality around money and provides personalized financial solutions that increase their engagement and overall financial health. By taking this quiz, users will gain insight and awareness that will point them in the right direction to tools and services that will further improve their financial well-being.

Role

Product designer
UX researcher
2023

Problem/challenge

The challenge is creating a digital experience that can analyze a user’s current behavior and engagement level with their prospective financial institutions. Doing so gives them recommendations to key banking products and services that will benefit their overall financial health.

Solution

This app aims to tackle the challenges that young adults often face in the banking industry. These challenges revolve around enhancing financial well-being, offering tailored advice, and addressing trust issues in banking. By honing in on the fundamental habits and behaviors that impact users' financial health, the app takes a holistic approach to deliver personalized solutions, directly addressing these pain points.

Insights

My biggest takeaway from my initial research is that young adults want to improve their overall financial wellbeing. They look at financially stable from more of a holistic view, not just earning a high income. There’s also investing, saving for retirement, and being prepared for uncertain emergencies. This explains the rise in fintech and non-banking app usage because these different channels can be used in tandem to target different financial needs.

Conclusion

The purpose of this app is to improve the financial health and financial engagement in young adults. Studies show that trust in traditional banking is fading, and the popularity in digital and nonbanking tool is growing because they solve the needs of it’s users. Millennials and Gen Z users are looking for finance products that they can trust, are fast, accessible, and improves their financial wellbeing. By creating a mobile app that is a personality test geared toward money and engagement, it solves the specific pain points that are consistently voiced by younger adults.
